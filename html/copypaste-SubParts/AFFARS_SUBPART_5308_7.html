SUBPART 5308.7 — ACQUISITION FROM NONPROFIT AGENCIES EMPLOYING PEOPLE WHO ARE BLIND OR SEVERELY DISABLED

Parent topic: PART 5308 - Required Sources of Supplies and Services

5308.705 Procedures

Refer to the Ability One Procurement Guide for acquiring products and services under 41 U.S.C., chapter 85 from nonprofit agencies employing people who are blind or severely disabled.
