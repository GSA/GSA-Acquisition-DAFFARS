5303.104-7 Violations or possible violations

(a) The contracting officer must provide a copy of the information and documentation generated under FAR 3.104-7 to their cognizant HCA, cognizant legal counsel, to SAF/AQC and to SAF/GCR.

(1) The contracting officer must forward the information and determination required by FAR 3.104-7(a)(1) to the clearance approval authority ( DAFFARS 5301.9001(i) ) of the affected procurement for review.

(f) See MP5301.601(a)(i)for individuals who may authorize award.
