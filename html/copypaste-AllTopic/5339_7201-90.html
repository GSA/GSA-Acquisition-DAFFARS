5339.7201-90 Data Servers/Centers Approval Process

Purchase Request packages for data servers/centers must include SAF/CIO A6 approval to be accepted as valid for procurement action by a contracting officer.

See DAFMAN17-1301 , Computer Security, COMPUSEC, paragraph 2.9.1.3 & 3.6.1
